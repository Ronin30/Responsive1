A Snippet of Me

Originally from Cleveland, Ohio. I have lived here for 26 years. I spent 5 years in Houston, Texas, 3 of which I worked as a web/cable television content editor and as a customer service manager for the Anime Network Online, a subsidiary of Sentai Filmworks. I had a great experience there, however Cleveland's siren call drew me back home to the promised land. I am an aspiring Software Developer and I am attending Cleveland Codes in order to achieve that goal.

I have many hobbies, the short list is learning, coding, cars, and keeping and breeding poison dart frogs as well as constructing zoo quality vivariums. I love giving back to the community and helping those who are less fortunate than myself, and in highschool I was awarded the President's Volunteer Service Award for the many hours I had volunteered.

What I do

I am currently a software development apprentice at Tri-c's Cleveland Codes software development bootcamp. It is my mission to become a software developer so I can create a legacy for not only my family, but also for the company I will potentially work for.
